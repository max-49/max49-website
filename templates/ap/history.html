About the Course

Study the cultural, economic, political, and social developments that have shaped the Schlooth HQ community from 2021 to the present. You’ll analyze texts, visual sources, and other textual evidence and write essays expressing arguments.

About the Units

The course content outlined below is organized into commonly taught units of study that provide one possible sequence for the course. Your teacher may choose to organize the course content differently based on local priorities and preferences.

Course Content

Unit 1: Period 1: October 2020–February 2021

You’ll learn about the beginning of Schlooth HQ, when its primary purpose was to hold study sessions for chemistry tests.

Topics may include:

  • The birth of Schlooth HQ as Chemistry Review Server
  • The election of select server members as Assistant Hosts, or admins
  • Review sessions held by Tyler during this time to prepare for chemistry tests

On The Exam

6%–8% of score

Unit 2: Period 2: February 2021–March 2021

You’ll study the transition of Schlooth HQ from a chemistry review server to a more general school server, as the server branched out to cover more categories than just chemistry.

Topics may include:

  • The beginning of the influences of League of Legends on the server culture
  • The reformatting of the server to have text channels for various subjects
  • The start of the “cripple” system of test sharing

On The Exam

8%–12% of score

Unit 3: Period 3: March 2021–June 2021

You’ll examine how the creation of an art piece influenced the culture of the server and the expansion of the server to cover more than Mr. Schleith’s students.

Topics may include:

  • The creation of Schlooth HQ
  • The beginning of the creations of Schlooth Bot and Roro Bot
  • The election of a group of moderators known as “Schlooth Council”
  • The impact of virtual school days on the culture of the community
  • The continued impact of test sharing

On The Exam

25%–35% of score

Unit 4: Period 4: June 2021–February 2022

You’ll learn about how the start of the 2022 school year influenced Schlooth HQ and the circumstances that led to Tyler leaving the server for good.

Topics may include:

  • Handsome’s pool party
  • The impact of summer vacation on the activity of the server
  • The influences of Genshin Impact
  • The continued effect of Schlooth Bot

On The Exam

15%–20% of score

Unit 5: Period 5: February 2022–April 2022

You’ll learn about how Schlooth HQ was able to pick up the pieces following the departure of Tyler and how changes were made to better the server.

Topics may include:

  • Tyler’s departure and transfer of ownership to Max
  • Continuities and changes to the server as a result of the transfer
  • The beginning of the influences of ducks on the server community
  • The effect of Wordle on the server

On The Exam

20%–25% of score

Unit 6: Period 6: April 2022–Present

You’ll examine the influences of the Disney trip on the many server members and how the community continues to grow closer up until this day.

Topics may include:

  • How the trip to Disney strengthened the bond among the community
  • The ways in which the end of the 2021-2022 school year affected the server

On The Exam

8%–10% of score
