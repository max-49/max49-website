ImaginaryCTF

ImaginaryCTF is a monthly CTF event where a new challenge is posted every day for the entirety of the month. At the end of each month, a new round starts. Imaginary CTF is mainly a beginner-oriented CTF with some harder challenges thrown into the mix later in the month.
